Where are the Dieties Now?

        We worship many dieties      There are 33 crores of dieties

Dieties are souls with divinity

But are God & dieties the same?

It is important to understand how each soul is on a journey. The soul descends from the eternal home during the Golden period and wears the bodily costume of a diety. The world was purest of pure. Nature was at its best. Dieties led a perfect life filled with health, wealth and happiness. They had unbelievable mental powers to achieve anything effortlessly. This was the outcome of practicising Rajyoga meditation in their previous birth during the Confluence age when the Supreme soul Himself imparted the knowledge. Such was their life that they knew no vices. They were worship worthy. The time is still remembered as Heaven, Paradise etc. Heaven is not a place, but is the time when souls were 100% pure. And it is this very earth, this very Bharat that was heaven. Only 9,00,000 souls took birth as dieties in the golden period.




But time turned and after 1250 years of Golden period, when the souls lost 20% of their purity they entered the Silver period. They were still dieties, but lost their original lustre. Also new souls kept descending from the soul world to be born during this period. This period lasted for 1250 years. And the population increased to 33 crores



By now the cycle completed 2500 years and the souls lost 50% of their purity & power as body consciousness set it and with it came the vices. Also population kept increasing. They started looking for God, worshipping and building grand temples in memory of the Supreme Father, Shiv. Then they built temples dedicated to the dieties. They did not remember that they were themselves the dieties in their previous births. This was the Copper period.
Time kept turning, and it changed to the Iron age, when human souls completely lost their purity and were under the influence of vices. The ones who were once dieties, now transformed into ordinary humans and that too sinners. Old age, sickness, death, sorrow, misery and darkness are the gifts of the negativities/vices inside us. When we lose our purity, we lose everything. This is the story of rise and fall of Bharat.



5000 years passed, and time has completed one complete cycle. Its time for rise of Bharat once again. Its the time of transformation. The time is the confluence between the end of the Iron & beginning of the Golden Period. This is the time when God decides to intervene. He descends on this earth. He talks through the mouth of Brahma, teaches the highest of high knowledge of Rajyoga meditation and awakens us. This is the only way to cleanse ourselves once again, and regain our lost glory of dietyhood. Its time for the next age-The Golden Age.


God is the Supreme soul, Highest of High, the unborn, always worship worthy and Ocean of purity permanently.


Dieties are pure & perfect human souls, who take birth in a body, leaves one body for another, possess divine qualities, becomes worship worthy, but temporarily.






Dark Aged Life

Dieties in Dark Age
Body consciousness
Started identifying oneself with the body

The same dieties who were naturally soul conscious, now became body conscious. They started thinking that one is the body that one can see. They started forgetting the ultimate truth that one is a soul wearing a body. Since the soul is not visible, they believed in what was visible-the body. They forgot that the body was perishable. They started seeking pleasure through the sense organs. They started seeing others as bodies too, noticed their appearances and identified them as bodies. Slowly they became gender conscious, and observed bodies as males and females. Gradually this gave birth to vices-lust, anger, greed, attachment and ego. With time, they considered this normal and lead lives of impurity. Soon, they lost their position of worship worthy pure dieties, and got demoted to normal human beings.

Conquered by vices
Under the grip of the vices

Vices are also known as Ravan, Maya, Satan, Devil, Mara etc. They do not exist outside. They are our inner weaknesses & negativities-fear, ego, lust, attachment, greed, anger, envy, comparison, doubt, revenge etc that are all born from our body consciousness. The major vice is lust, and then the rest follows it. Since the Copper period, the human souls lost their purity and hence lost control of their own sense organs, their own mind. They indulged in vices as their minds were overpowered, and committed sins after sins. The worst part was that, they did not realise they were wrong and considered the vices to be normal. They lost the power to distinguish good from evil. Their life style changed and slowly degraded. They forgot themselves as souls, and as a result forgot the Father of all souls, God, thus losing divinity and falling from grace. They are given names such as Asur, Lucifer, Hades, PLuto, Hel, Melek Taus etc. Heaven turned to Hell

Lost all powers
Helplessness, despair, sorrow, darkness..

Once upon a time, the dieties were brimming with inner powers and led a life of complete happiness. Now the same dieties started experiencing pain, weakness, powerlessness, confusion, tension, stress and lost all their wisdom. Oldage, sickness, death, sorrow and darkness took over their lives. They sought solace by remembering God, building temples, going for piligrimages, doing some good deeds, writing scriptures. However they did not find any relief as they did not know who their real Father was. They forgot who they were. Body consciousness, the root cause of all misery started showing its true colors. Even then no one realised who the enemy was. As the enemy was hidden inside us. The sense organs took over, became the master and started controlling them. The dieties lost all consciousness. The world turned dark (Night of Brahma) and is called Kalyug.

Lost all virtues
From 16 celestial degrees full to none

Shri Krishna, the first diety is still remembered as 16 celestial degrees full. He was the first complete and perfect human being on earth. When the dieties were soul conscious they radiated peace, love, happiness, bliss, power, wisdom and purity. They had divine qualities. They were talented and every virtue was reflected in their actions. They were naturally skilled in Arts, Science, Maths and creativity. They never trained or learned to acquire wisdom. However as they became body conscious, lost their purity, they degraded and lost all their divine virtues. They were later shown as dark/blue skinned as vices are like poison for the soul. It is not the skin color but the dark layer in the soul. The soul was stained with vices. They found themselves in a world of crimes, murder, rape, adultery, robbery-also referred to as "hell".

Became Worshippers
Seeking solace by worshipping

The same dieties who were once worship worthy now changed. Since they lost their inner power, they started seeking and calling out to the Supreme Father. The first temple in Somnath was built in remembrance of God, the Supreme soul with no physical body. The Shiv Linga is just a symbol of the bodiless Father of all souls. The dieties started creating rituals, wrote scriptures, created pilgrimages and started doing welfare related deeds. However, this gave only momentary peace. Hence they started worshipping the ones who dwelt in heaven or in the Golden period in the form of ancestors. They could not remember that they are the same souls. Thats why they say "pujya so pujari", "Hum so so hum".

Transformation Age

  • God hears us

    As mentioned in Bhagavita Gita, God comes once at the end of each cycle of 5000 years, to liberate all souls, and take us back home. We called out to Him since the Copper age. However He only descends on this earth when the world is in utter chaos, when all souls get stained with sins and the world becomes hell. He heard us, and is here now. One who has completed their devotion will come and recognize Him. He highlights the causes of sorrow, and provides the remedy. Whoever follows the remedies can be cleansed and can accompany Him to our sweet home, and then return back to heaven as dieties. He is not a human soul, but the highest of High and the Father of all souls-the Supreme soul. He is never born, hence has no body like us. He is the most powerful energy, and resembles a dot that cannot be viewed with our physical eyes.

  • Prajapita Brahma

    God does not have parents and does not wear a physical costume called body. However He descends on this earth to purify all the souls and create heaven. He shares the mysteries of the universe as He is the Ocean of Knowledge. Now He needs a body so that He can perform his duties. He selects the body of the first soul Krishna, who is now reborn as an ordinary human being named Lekhraj Kripalini. He was a family man, a successful diamond merchant and a pious human being. He himself was unaware of his previous births. The Supreme soul speaks through his mouth and reminds him all about his journey from the Golden period as Shree Krishna, and to now as a regular person. As God chooses Lekhraj Kripalini as his instrument, He renames him as Prajapita Brahma-the first born of God and the beginning of human creation.

  • Rajyoga Meditation

    To change from hell to heaven, from impure to pure, from humans to dieties and from imperfect to perfect, God advocates "purity". Purity not only means celibacy but purity in thoughts, words and deeds. Only complete purity can bring back peace and prosperity. To achieve that, God teaches Rajyog meditation by speaking through the mouth of Brahma. It is the science of mastering ourselves and becoming the king of our senses. The Supreme soul, whom we lovingly address as "Shiv Baba" is the Ocean of Perfection. When we remember Him, direct our thoughts towards Him and focus on His virtues we the souls experience transfer of energy from Him that empowers us to fight against the vices and come out as winners. Dussehra and Vijay dashami are celebrated to commemorate this event of our victory.

  • Battle against vices

    The root cause of impurity in us is body consciousness. With body consciousness comes the major vices-lust, anger, greed, attachment & ego. Heaven became hell only because of our impurities that dwell in us. The only way out is to fight the negativities in us and wipe them out. The first step is to acknowledge our real enemy, the inner demons. The second step is to tackle the root cause and practice soul consciousness. The third step is to meditate on our Father, the Ocean of purity. The fourth step is to imbibe the Godly knowledge and use them as weapons. By doing this our inner powers that were left unused gets revived and our demonaic traits gradually get replaced by the divinity hidden within us. However the battle is tough and long. Eventually the soul wins. Thats why 9 days of festival is celebrated and the 10th day is the day of victory. That is the battle of the soul against demons of vices.

  • Human to Diety

    We changed from deities to humans to devils and so did the world. The world changed from heaven to hell. If we change, the world changes. As we practice Rajyoga meditation, remember God our Supreme Father, listen to the Godly knowledge and imbibe it, share the knowledge with others, follow all the dictates of the Supreme Father our life changes. Our divinity that was merged within us starts to emerge gradually. Our life changes and the transformation starts showing. We still live in this world yet not affected by the impurities just like a lotus flower blooming in mud. Our old habits and beliefs get replaced by new. The mind gets stronger and stable. Slowly humans improve and get closer to dietyhood. They are awakened.

Hurdles in the Path

The path is difficult but not impossible

Our old habits that are stamped into the subconscious mind may pose as a barrier. We may keep getting drawn to our old ways as that comes naturally to us now.

If we do not prioritize spirituality and keep pushing it for later, or keep postponing it or keep giving excuses, that may pose as a hurdle.

When we see transformation happening slowly or see no changes at all, we may get disheartened and may want to give up.

Since we still live in the old world, we keep getting pulled towards it, get lured by the shortterm attractiveness, get pressurised by family&friends to give up.

Why is the path so slippery, and steep?

Keep moving ahead carefully as the goal is not too far
