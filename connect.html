Connection with our Source

        Rajyog Meditation is connecting with our Source      Meeting of child and parent

It is the union of the soul and the Supreme soul

Taught By God Himself

We now know that we are souls. And our eternal Father is the Supreme soul. Rajyog is the process of uniting with our Father. The word "yog" means to add or to join. We become like the one we focus on. When the soul gets lost in remembrance of the Supreme Soul, the qualities of God gets transferred to us. Also our own virtues gets activated. This empowers us to master our senses and become "Raja" or the king of our own self. We gain control.




Prayer is a one sided communication between the child and Father, where the child recites a prayer, or sings a hymn, or reads verses from the holy books. However these prayers, verses or hymns are written by some great devotees who experienced God. When we repeat them, we do not understand the meaning of the powerful words and just recite them like parrots. Hence we fail to feel the love and affection for God.

Rajyog meditation is a two sided communication. Here the soul or the child feels close to the Father and feels immense love for Him. In return we experience the deep and unconditional love of God or our Father. This fills us with supersensous joy. The soul is rejuvenated and now our happiness is beyond our senses. This means we no longer seek approval, support, appreciation, love.. And we do not need any reason to be happy.

We tried to connect with great souls by meeting them, conversing with them or by listening to them. We visited places of worship. We went for pilgrimages. When we returned, we felt positive and vibrant. However it was shortlived. This is because no soul with a physical body is powerful enough to empower another soul permanently. We need to connect with the perfect soul and that is the Supreme soul to gain everlasting happiness.

In the past we tried to reach God through someone be it a diety or a guru or a priest. In Rajyoga meditation we connect directly to the source.

It is believed in India that going for pilgrimages and bathing in holy rivers can purify us. Going for pilgrimages may give us temporary satisfaction of fulfulling our duties towards God. Dipping our bodies in rivers can only clean the body. Performing rituals can only keep us occupied for sometime. But to cleanse ourselves we need to dip ourself in the Ocean of Purity. Only by connecting with the Ever Pure Supreme soul, can the soul regain its lost purity. And this is Rajyog meditation.






Steps of Rajyog Meditation

5 STEPS TO PRACTICE RAJYOG
Relaxation
Be Comfortable physically and mentally

Sit in a comfortable position either on the ground, or on a chair. Relax your body completely starting from your toes, to knees, to stomach, to shoulders, to neck to your facial muscles. Release all your tensions, stress and heaviness. Forget all your problems and keep them aside for some time. Feel as if you are home, your true home where you can totally relax. Take deep breaths. Feel as it everything is good and there is no need to worry. Preferred time is early morning 4-5AM, and evening 6-8PM as the atmosphere is less noisy.

Concentration
See yourself as a tiny star in the center of your forehead

Remember your original identity. You are not a body but the soul. And you are sitting in the center of your forehead to control the body. You look like a sparkling diamond. That is who you are. Visualise your body and realise that this is not you. It is only the costume that you are wearing to express yourself. While focussing, thoughts of work, relations, people etc would come to divert your attention. Calmly observe them and understand they are not true. Watch them leave and continue with the thought. You are bodiless.You are a soul.

Contemplation
Reflect on yourself by going deeper

The nature of the mind is to think continuously. Do not build a dam and do not force it. Steer it lovingly in the right direction and let it think. But this time you are the one who guides the mind to create the thoughts. Contemplate on your original nature of love, purity, bliss, happiness, knowledge, power and peace. See yourself in your sweet silence home as a soul. The soul world is bright golden red in color. It welcomes you with deep peaceful vibrations. Feel the peace. Feel the silence. Feel the worryfree atmosphere. Feel the deep relief of finally being at home. Feel yourself as a point of light. Completely bodiless.

Realization
Finally you realize your true self

You are the soul separate from your body. You are way above the material world. You are at home. Get lost in this incorporeal world, true home of all souls, highest of high abode where only bodiless souls reside. Fly higher in this world and observe other souls sparkling and set in their positions. Keep flying higher and higher while absorbing the pure vibrations. You reached the topmost point and now you see the brightest of bright star sparkling with radiance. He is our Father. The Supreme soul. The Father of all souls. Our imperishable Father. Watch Him and feel his unconditional love.

Meditation
Realize the source

Visualize the soul world and see the source at the topmost position. Focus on Him, get closer and observe. See that he is bodiless like me. He has no body. He radiates unconditonal acceptance, love and welcomes me with pure affection with His powerful vibrations. He is filled with peace and loves me without any reason. I feel deep love for my Father. I am happy to meet Him. I am receiving powerful energy from Him that seems to be empowering me. The stains of sin, sorrow, despair, guilt, hopelessness seems be getting wiped out. My own purity is reflecting itself and I am glowing with love. I can feel my vibrations of love and peace spreading all around like him. Slowly I start feeling lighter, cleaner and purer.


















Techniques of Rajyog Meditation

  • Going Within

    Rajyog meditation does not need any recitation of sacred words, chanting, singing hymns, saying prayers, repetition of any name, pranayama, or any physical act. All our life we never paid any attention to our thoughts. We let them come, carry us away wherever it wants and make us distracted. We never tried to control them or ever worried about the quality of our thoughts. We always thought it did not affect us anyway. But it does. Now the mind is out of control and drags us away with negative and unnecessary thoughts. The thoughts now behave like children all grown up and wild because no discipline was taught during their childhood. Rajyog is all inner work. The technique is all about the mind, intellect, thoughts & how to work on them.

  • Distant Observer

    When we talk about observing, it is not about the physical eyes. About the physical eyes, we keep them open as we communicate with our Supreme Father and any communication does not involve closing eyes. Also as we connect with the divine, positive vibrations are transmitted through the eyes purifying the atmosphere. Now when we sit to meditate, thoughts will come to interrupt us. Observe them come as if they are uninvited guests. Look at them from a distance with your mind's eye as if they are not yours. As if you are separate from the thoughts. Keep a guard on them as they continue to come. Guide the mind to think about the real you. At the same time observe the stray thoughts. Do not let them stay. Do not let them pull you away. Be on guard. Instead replace them with thoughts about the real you. The soul. Watch the negative thoughts disappear.

  • Understanding the Power of mind

    As you think so you become. Everything that you see around you is a manifestation of what you thought in the past. Everything that comes to you good or bad is a result of how you thought in the past. Thoughts have vibrations that radiate into the universe and returns back to you manifold whether you want or not. Knowing this, we can now change the mind from running in auto control to manual control. We can create thoughts consciously that can benefit us and everyone around us. We take control and ensure that we replace all negative and waste thoughts into positive and elevated thoughts. What are positive and elevated thoughts? They are thoughts about universal welfare, love and acceptance for all souls. They are thoughts beyond the body and the physical world and realisation of being a soul. They are thoughts about our creator who is beyond perfection and the more we think about Him we start becoming like Him. They are thoughts about the knowledge delivered by the Supreme soul directly through His human medium.

  • Relationship with the divine

    We make friends with whom we share similar thinking, habits and tastes. In Rajyoga meditation we build a relationship with God, the Supreme soul, the Highest of High. This is not an ordinary thought. Only the fortunate ones get to befriend God Himself. But God Himself offers his hand to us for friendship. However we must be similar to enjoy our friendship. God is the purest of pure, inexperienced about vices and all kinds of negativity. To accept His hand, we must become a new person with new habits and filled with positivity. The most elevated soul is repelled by the five main vices-lust, anger, greed, attachment, ego. So to become God's friend we need to make some changes in our life. The soul itself is viceless but acquired the vices after taking many births. The practice to realize oneself as a soul will take one closer to God. Because He is bodiless.

  • Recharging everyday

    The soul has been on a journey of many human births. Each birth the soul gets a new physical costume, new relations, new life to perform the new role. As soon as the role gets over, the soul leaves for another new body. This has been continued for maximum 5000 years and for maximum 84 births. Now the soul is in the last leg of its journey. During this process the soul is drained out of its energy and needs replenishment. However the energy must come from the Supreme Charger who is forever charged & energized, who never gets into the cycle of births & deaths and who is only capable of rejuvenating us. This is Rajyog meditation when I the soul connect to the Supreme Charger and recharge myself. However it is not a one time process. It has to be regularly and diligently practiced. Just as a mobile needs to be recharged each day to function properly, so I the soul need to be connected to my Source each day.

  • Join the Godly University

    Rajyog meditation is the deepest spiritual science and one requires guidance. One has to learn the techniques correctly in order to get the desired results. Just as a child gets admission in a school in order to start their academic life. The child is exposed to a proper atmosphere that aids learning, teachers to guide them and companions to learn together. One must join the nearest Brahmakumari center and start their spiritual education in a formal way. Rajyoga meditation is not a regular "satsang" to come and listen to religious discourses, like in other places. This is spiritual education that one receives and the teacher is none other than God Himself. Here it is not about reading from scriptures, but live classes of meditation and spiritual studies. Here it is about implementing the learnings in our real life. So it is named as Godly University for deep spiritual education that can transform our lives.
