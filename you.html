The ability to step back and disengage from the world around you

Power to Assess

Power to Decide

Power to Cooperate

Power to Accomodate

Instead of revealing unnecessary, negative or personal information of others, this power helps one to accomodate thereby winning the trust of others.

Power to Packup

Always everready whenever called, as one knows how to pack up instantly. One can quickly put a fullstop to things and does not allow oneself to be diverted or distracted.

My Habits or Sansksar

How Are Habits Formed??

Another word for habits are "Impressions". When any action is repeated multiple times, it forms an impression in the mind. The act gets imprinted and stamped in the subconscious or the deepest layer of the mind


Now that it is embedded in the subconscious mind, next time the same action does not need conscious effort. It becomes automatic and natural. Now its my habit. Or its my "sanskar"


When a person smoked for the first time, it did not become a habit. However when the person keeps repeating the act whether he enjoys it or not simply becomes a habit as the impression has formed in the subconscious mind. It is the same with good habits!
The soul has three faculties: Mind, Intellect and Habit

Mind is the first faculty of the soul that creates thoughts continuously. Brain is a part of the body and not soul. It is a machine that aids the soul as long as it lasts. If not trained it will run wild and create thoughts naturally as per the previous habits of the soul.

Intellect is the second faculty that creates visuals as per the thoughts coming from the mind. It can provide suggestions, gives advices and is called the conscience or "vivek"

If the intellect passes any act from the second faculty, and if the act is performed many times, it gets stored as a habit in the soul, very popularly known as "karma". This travels with the soul till the next body if stored quite deep.

Each soul has carried habits from 5 places

Habits carry forwarded from past births, will impact our current life

Habits acquired when in the family will also influence our present life

Habits acquired during interactions with friends, relatives and other external environment

Habits created out of sheer will power, determination and strong discipline can help form new habits

Habits that are naturally inherent in the soul, or the original virtues-purity, peace, love, bliss, happiness, power & wisdom
